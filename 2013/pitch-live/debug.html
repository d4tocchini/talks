Using AI to Scale Good Design

On the Grid, sites design themselves to adapt the content, "content dictates form." This is accomplished orchestrating several Constraint Solvers.

Constraint Programming is a paradigm that originated in AI in the 1970s, and more recently, has been the secret sauce in Search.

Cassowary Linear Arithmetic Constraint Solver

paper ★ github

PhD project of our friend, Greg Badros, currently VP at Facebook.

Unnoticed for a decade until Apple integrated in OS X and iOS.

The Grid is the first to perfect & commercialize it for the web.
